Characters

Tre'Merdil

He's sincere, thoughtful, savvy and perhaps a little too adventurest. He was born in a decent family in a small village in Everir. He lived free of worries until he was about 12 years old, but at that point things changed. He started to travel for the local community and was learning a lot of new skills. Through hard work and with his charm, there's nothing to stop him from going beyond expectations. He came quickly become a person of great importance. He is currently still learning, exploring and discovering. He feels like there's more people to meet in this world.

Difficulty: Intermediate

Cards

Speciality

If the player rolled 3 or above on their turn, can roll the dice again. Each turn can be used once.

Yali

She's cute, insane and obsessive. She was born and grew up in a lower class family in a wealthy town in Everir. She maimed somebody during a revolution and was initiated in a gang. With the help of a suspicious stranger she had to survive in a cruel world. But with her talents and skills, she managed to survive everything and overcome all odds. Still plagued by the past, she now works as killer for hire. By doing so, she hopes to learn more about the past and finally find pleasureful life she has never had.

Difficulty: Beginner

Cards

Speciality

The opponent cannot walk the turn after it is used. They can walk after using “Walker” . They can push-pull anyone or may teleport.

Mardomir

He's fraud, rogue and conceited. Which isn't out of the ordinary for someone with his gruesome past. He was born and grew up in a lower class family in a merchant capital in Everir, he lived free of worries until he was about 12 years old, but at that point things took a turn for the worst. He accidently conned somebody noble during a suspicious accident and was forsaken by all. But with his intelligence and experience, he managed to train to perfection and escape hell.

Difficulty: Advanced

Cards

Specialities

The opponent plays the next turn with one dice. When 3 or less dice are rolled, +5 additional damage applies.

The opponent plays the next turn with one card of they choice. The card must be selected before the turn starts.

The lives of the opponent and the player are exchanged.

Azdofaz

He's cautious, self-reliant and cruel. This is to be expected from somebody with his origin. He was born and grew up in a assassin family in a abandoned city in Zafoza. He lost his community in a war and left alone. Without any help he had to survive in a mad world. This has turned him into the shadow he is today.

Difficulty: Intermediate

Cards

Speciality

Teleport to any square next to opponent. The first hit deals +5 additional damage. Cannot be used after walking. Cannot be used next to opponent. Cannot be used after another support card has been used.

Caju'Kah

He's angry, sadist and war enthusiast. He was born and grew up in a high noble class family in a mountain capital city in Kuahhak. He lived full of pain. He lost his arms a in war. After new special metal arms were made for it, he became invincible from close fights. While still constantly on fighting, he now works for his people. By doing so, he hopes to gain rich lands he has never had.

Difficulty: Beginner

Cards

Speciality

All damage dealt to the opponent adds to the player's life.

Akdahal

She's petulant, brave and pensive. But this is all just a facade, a mechanism to deal with her terrible past. She was born and grew up in a lower class family in an average community in Kuahhak. She lost her mother in a fight which got out of control and was headed for a life of crime. Without any help she had to survive in a crazy world. But with her bravery and capability, she managed to face all obstacles and go beyond expectations. This has turned her into the warrior she is today. While still constantly on the move, she now tracking the people of her past.

Difficulty: Beginner

Cards

Speciality

The opponent cannot use any distance damage cards on the next turn. Any support cards can be used.

Igburis

She's a dangerous and generous shepherd. She was born in deep forest in Suguis. She lived extraordinary life in nature. She lost her siblings in a terrible disaster. Against all odds she had to survive in a pitiless world. But with her intrepidness and vigor, she managed to protect forest and sprout new souls. Despite all threats she is overcome, currently still trying to make the world perfect.

Difficulty: Advanced

Cards

Specialities

Summon a character Life: 4, Close Damage: 2, Walk Point: 3 The summoned character continues to live until they killed. After the player's turn ends, the character is directed. The player can direct the character as they wish. Cannot be summoned same character before the character dies. The character can only be killed in one turn. If the character does not die after taking damage, character’s life is restored to the next turn. The character cannot finish his turn next the to player.

Summon a character Life: 2, Close Damage: 1, Walk Point: 8 The summoned character continues to live until they killed. After the player's turn ends, the character is directed. The player can direct the character as they wish. Cannot be summoned same character before the character dies. The character can only be killed in one turn. If the character does not die after taking damage, character’s life is restored to the next turn. The character cannot finish his turn next the to player.

Omoperdon

He's strong, primitive and savage. This is to be expected from somebody from northern people. He was born and grew up in abandoned village in a hunter family, he lived full of danger and became a great warriror in Epomenon. He lost his brother in a war and was headed for a life of crime. Against all odds he had to survive in a cruel world. But with his power and potency, he managed to make a new home and overcome all odds. This has turned him into the hunter he is today.

Difficulty: Beginner

Cards

Speciality

Adding +3 additional walking. It can be use before or after walking. It can be used after any card has been used.

Gibuza

He's conceited, angry and dauntless. But there's more than meets the eye. He was born and grew up in a fairly noble family in a broken town in Everir, he lived without worry until he was about 16 years old, but at that point life took a turn for the worst. He killed a lot of people from gangs and being targeted by everybody. But with his powers and skills, he managed to fight anyone who gets in his way and overpower anybody who's a hinderance.

Difficulty: Intermediate

Cards

Speciality

The player can pull or push their opponent 5 square away. If the opponent pulled, must have to come next to the player. If the opponent is to be pushed, up to 5 square can be finished to the any distance.

Erp'Idayo

He's skillful, confident and brave. But what'd you expect from somebody with his level. He was born and grew up in an average family in an important community in Everir. He try to protect and live in peace in his city. Settled down and with some peace and quiet, he now works as a guard with perfecting skills and talents. By doing so, he hopes to be released of the haunting memories and finally find happiness he has never had.

Difficulty: Beginner

Cards

Speciality

Player can teleport to the corner where the opponent is close. If the opponent is standing in the corner, it can starts from any of the next square. Cannot be used after walk. Cannot be used next to the opponent.

Gisubut

He's gloomy, sorcerous and quiet. This is to be expected from someone of his race. He was born and grew up in dark forest with an outsider tribe who remain silent in Suguis. He lived in shadows until his tribe discovered by foreigners, at that point everything changed. He lost his most of the tribe in an epidemic and was caught up with the wrong people. He was try to calm himself for a long time until he found someone from his tribe. His skills and capability, he managed to battle and crush all that's in the way.

Difficulty: Intermediate

Cards

Speciality

The player is teleported to the square where they started the game. The player can continue from there. Can be used after any other card. Cannot be walk after using.

Beri'Vodel

She's affectionate, agile, popular and perhaps a little too savvy. She was born in a high class family in an important community in Everir. She lived free of worries until she was about 14 years old, but at that point things changed. She traveled to Suguis and start to working for the merchant union and protecting their some great threats. With the support of great friends, she managed to thrive in a wild world. But with her brilliance and sense of humor, there's nothing to stop her from staying ahead of the game. She came quickly become a person of great leader.

Difficulty: Advanced

Cards

Specialities

The player can walk their opponent anywhere up to 3 steps.

The lives of the opponent and the player are equalized. The lower life is applied.
